# Content Style Guide Exceptions Log

Per `docs/content-style-guide.md` §24, any exception to the style guide must be granted in writing, per post, and logged here.

**Owner**: Content Relay · **Slack**: `#content-ops-alerts`

## How to log an exception

Add a new entry at the top of the log, newest first. Use the template below.

```md
### YYYY-MM-DD — <Post title or URL>
- **Domain**: sighthound.com | redactor.com
- **Rule waived**: <section number and rule, e.g. §7 banned word "leverage">
- **Reason**: <1–2 sentences on why the rule cannot be followed for this post>
- **Granted by**: <Content Relay owner name>
- **Expires**: <date or "permanent for this URL">
- **Mitigations**: <what we did instead to keep the intent of the rule>
```
